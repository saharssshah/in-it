How to Play

How To Enter The Prize Competitions

  1. Pick the Competition you would like to enter.
  2. Select the ticket number/s you would like in the Competition.
  3. Answer the required question correctly.
  4. Pay for your entries, or receive a Lucky Dip for free by tapping the link above the timer on each competition page and following the instructions.

How To Enter The Prize Competitions

  1. After all the numbers are sold out, we will announce the draw time and date on our social media page.
  2. We post all draw numbers on our pages.
  3. Our live stream draw will use google random number generator to pick the number, a lucky winner will then be picked.

  1. The prize will be delivered to the winner within 7 days of the winner being announced. Delivery is free to UK.
